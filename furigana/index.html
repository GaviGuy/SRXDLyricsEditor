Romaji or furigana goes here. Check for accuracy, pacing, etc.
Special formatting symbols:

  (space): indicates the start of a new syllable (and a new kana).
| (pipe): indicates the start of a new pronounced word.
* (asterisk): indicates a kana where no pronunciation should go.


As an example, the first Japanese line from Lovesick.
I first put this line into the top box:
諦めましょうこの人生

For the next box, I need to match every kana with a pronunciation.
Here's the first step, romaji:
akiramemashou kono jinsei

Next, to assist the program, I will put pipes between each word,
and spaces between each kana's pronunciation:
akira me ma sh o u | ko no | jin sei

Notice how the number of kana and the number of pronounced chunks match.

With both boxes populated, I can click "Copy Output to Clipboard" and
paste into the Spin Rhythm lyric editor.
